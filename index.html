About Lynnette Jaiswal

TOP-RATED LUXURY REALTOR
IN SAN DIEGO

Lynnette Jaiswal is the founder and principal broker of The Jaiswal Group Real Estate — a boutique luxury real estate firm specializing in buying, selling, and new construction homes throughout North San Diego County's most sought-after communities, including Carmel Valley, Pacific Highlands Ranch, 4S Ranch, Del Mar, Solana Beach, Rancho Santa Fe, Encinitas, Carlsbad, and beyond.

Since 2019, Lynnette has been recognized as one of the Top 3% of REALTORS® in San Diego County by the North San Diego County Association of REALTORS®, with over $75M in career sales. She holds 14 professional designations — including CLHMS Guild, MCNE, RENE, and GRI — and serves on the Professional Standards Committee for the North San Diego County Association of REALTORS®.

Whether you're searching for luxury resale homes, exploring new construction communities, or preparing to sell for top dollar, Lynnette's approach is grounded in one principle: relationships over transactions. Every client receives a tailored strategy and white-glove service from first consultation through close.
