Before You List

KEY QUESTIONS TO CONSIDER

Before putting your home on the market, Lynnette walks every seller through these critical questions to make sure you're set up for success.

Do I know what my home is actually worth?

Online estimates like Zestimates are notoriously inaccurate — sometimes off by 10–20%. A personalized Comparative Market Analysis from Lynnette gives you a real number based on actual recent sales in your neighborhood.

Is my home ready to show?

First impressions drive offers. Lynnette will walk through your home and provide a customized pre-listing plan — from decluttering and staging to repairs that deliver the highest return on investment.

Am I clear on my net proceeds?

Understanding your closing costs, agent commissions, and any outstanding mortgage balance helps you plan what comes next — whether that's buying another home or relocating entirely.

Where am I going after I sell?

Selling without a plan for your next move can create stress and rushed decisions. Lynnette helps coordinate your timeline so selling and buying align seamlessly.

Do I understand today's market conditions?

Is it a buyer's or seller's market right now? How many days are homes sitting? What's the list-to-sale ratio in your neighborhood? Lynnette briefs every seller on market conditions before making any decisions.

Do I have a marketing strategy?

Price it right. Photograph it beautifully. Promote it everywhere. The Jaiswal Group's marketing plan combines professional photography, social media, targeted digital advertising, and the MLS to maximize exposure.
