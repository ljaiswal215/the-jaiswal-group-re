ABOUT THIS COMMUNITY

ARABELLA AT PACIFIC HIGHLANDS RANCH

Arabella is a collection of 132 two-story, single-family residences developed by Pardee Homes in 2005 — one of Pacific Highlands Ranch's original and most beloved communities. Set less than 4.5 miles from the Pacific Ocean, the neighborhood's seven streets — Shasta Daisy Trail, Ginger Glen Trail, Cornflower Trail, Moonflower Meadows Trail, Cape Jewels Trail, Painted Nettles Glen, and Cullen Crest Trail — connect residents seamlessly to the broader Pacific Highlands Ranch trail network and the community life around them.

Daily life in Arabella is defined by genuine walkability. Schools, the community park, and The Village at Pacific Highlands Ranch — with its dining, boutiques, and specialty grocery — are all within easy reach on foot. Arabella homeowners also enjoy exclusive access to the West Highland Pacific Recreation Center, featuring a Junior Olympic pool, spa, fitness room, and water play area that serve as a true neighborhood gathering point.

Homes range from 2,007 to 2,453 square feet with 3 to 4 bedrooms and lot sizes up to 0.15 acres. Arabella is governed by the West Highlands Pacific HOA, which oversees the community’s shared amenities and common areas — including the private recreation center that residents enjoy year-round.

FREQUENTLY ASKED

ABOUT ARABELLA

What is Arabella at Pacific Highlands Ranch?

Arabella is a community of 132 single-family homes in Pacific Highlands Ranch, San Diego (ZIP 92130), developed by Pardee Homes in 2005. Homes range from 2,007 to 2,453 square feet with 3 to 4 bedrooms. Residents enjoy exclusive access to the West Highland Pacific Recreation Center, direct connectivity to PHR’s trail network, and walkable proximity to The Village at Pacific Highlands Ranch.

What schools are zoned for Arabella?

Students in Arabella attend Solana Ranch Elementary, Pacific Trails Middle School, and either Canyon Crest Academy or Torrey Pines High School — within the Solana Beach School District and San Dieguito Union High School District.

How far is Arabella from the beach?

Arabella sits less than 4.5 miles from the Pacific Ocean, giving residents easy access to Del Mar’s beaches and the mild, marine-influenced climate that defines life in coastal San Diego. It’s inland enough for generous lot sizes, yet close enough to feel the ocean breeze every day.

Is Arabella part of an HOA?

Yes. Arabella is governed by the West Highlands Pacific HOA, which oversees shared amenities including the private recreation center with a Junior Olympic pool, spa, fitness room, and water play area. Reach out to us for current HOA fee information and governing documents.
