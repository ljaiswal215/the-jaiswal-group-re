The Difference

WHY A PERSONAL VALUATION BEATS AN ONLINE ESTIMATE

Zillow, Redfin, and other automated tools work from public data and broad algorithms — they can't see your upgraded kitchen, your custom built-ins, or the quiet cul-de-sac that buyers are willing to pay a premium for. Studies show these estimates can miss the mark by 5–15% or more, which on a San Diego home can mean six figures of difference. A personal valuation from The Jaiswal Group accounts for your home's specific features, real current buyer demand, and local market nuance — giving you an accurate number you can actually make decisions with.
